Overview

Purpose

To provide information about and advertising for past, current, and future litters from Rigby Doodles breeding company

Audience

Families

Dynamic elements

Litters and puppies and relevant information are contained in an object-array that can be easily modified for future litters. Past Litters can be sorted by breed and parent dogs using a drop down filter/search bar. Modal that opens up on image click with more detailed information about each puppy.
